About me

Hi! My name is Kelly and I am a first-generation Mexican-American. Although born in the U.S., Mexico has been my second home. I am very fond and proud of seeing where my parents came from and how Mexican culture is part of me as well. I created this project to share the beauty and magic of Mexico and all its culture, as well as practicing the opportunity to improve my coding skills.
